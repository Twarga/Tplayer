Tplayer
Features Download GitHub

Your music,
beautifully grounded.

A polished local-first desktop music player. Fast playback, clean queue control, imported tracks beside your albums, and a warm dark interface.

Download for Linux View Source

Also available for Windows. Built with Electron and React.

Your library, not a dashboard

Tplayer drops the aggressive algorithmic recommendations and filler home pages. It treats your local music folders as first-class citizens with a fast, deeply searchable library view and a layout focused on albums and playback.

Expand directly from YouTube

Can't find a track locally? Search YouTube directly from inside Tplayer, download the audio via embedded yt-dlp, and watch it seamlessly join your library alongside your offline files.

Integrated deeply

Built for heavy daily listening with global media keys, full MPRIS support on Linux desktop environments, a 10-band equalizer with presets, and reliable Last.fm scrobbling.
